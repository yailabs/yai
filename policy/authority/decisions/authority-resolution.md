# Authority Resolution

Authority resolution composes domain sensitivity and compliance constraints.

## Resolution cues

- Domain risk profile determines baseline authority sensitivity.
- Compliance layers can increase required scope.
- Sector overlays can require explicit approval or dual control.

## Examples

- Digital external publication with GDPR personal-data context -> review escalation to operator+auditor.
- Scientific high-risk AI Act context -> controller scope + human oversight trace.
- Economic high-impact transaction context with finance overlay -> dual control before high-impact release.
