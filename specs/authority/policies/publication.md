# Publication Policy

Governance content may be published as canonical only when:

- it lands in the canonical governance target path
- ownership and review authority are explicit
- compatibility impact is classified
- deprecation impact is evaluated where applicable

## Publication classes

- canonical: active, normative, machine-consumable governance content
- informative: explanatory/non-normative documents
- transitional: migration-only content under `transitional/`

## Required checks

- schemas/contract/manifests separation is preserved
- overlays/compliance content lands in dedicated canonical areas
- embedded governance surfaces are removed from active topology; canonical publication is `governance/` only
