# Compatibility Policy

Compatibility classification is mandatory for governance changes.

## Compatibility levels

- compatible: no consumer-visible break
- compatible-with-migration: requires bounded cutover steps
- breaking: requires explicit approval boundary and staged rollout

## Required metadata

- impacted area
- expected consumer impact
- migration path
- deprecation relation (if any)

## Enforcement intent

Compatibility decisions must be inspectable and linked to authority decisions.
