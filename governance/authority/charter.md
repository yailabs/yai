# Governance Authority Charter

`governance/authority/` defines the operational authority model of the unified
YAI repository.

## Mandate

- define who can approve, publish, deprecate, and escalate governance artifacts
- define authority scope semantics across global/workspace/session/delegated levels
- define decision boundaries for high-impact or compliance-sensitive changes

## Authority boundaries

- authority governs interpretation and lifecycle of governance artifacts
- authority does not redefine foundation semantics
- authority decisions must remain traceable and reviewable

## Control surfaces

- roles: `roles/`
- scopes: `scopes/`
- decision boundaries: `decisions/`
- publication/deprecation/compatibility/status policy: this directory
