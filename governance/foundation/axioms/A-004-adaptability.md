# A-004 — Governed Adaptability

Adaptability is allowed only inside governance constraints.

`brain` is the native cognitive plane for adaptation.
`brain` is not sovereign authority.

## Normative statements

- Cognitive reconfiguration MUST be explicit, attributable, and authority-bound.
- Invalid cognitive assumptions MUST trigger suspension or constrained continuation under explicit authority.
- Adaptation MUST NOT silently authorize external effects.
- Reconfiguration records MUST connect invalidation, old/new cognitive configuration, and authority basis for resumption.

## Non-goals

Adaptability is not arbitrary autonomy.
Adaptability is not a bypass of policy, authority, or effect boundaries.

A system that adapts without governance is non-conformant.
