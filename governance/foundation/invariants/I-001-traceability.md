# I-001 — Traceability

Traceability is mandatory across `core`, `exec`, and `brain`.

## Invariant

Every governed transition MUST be attributable, reconstructable, and explainable with evidence sufficient for offline verification.

## Mandatory properties

- authority reference and decision basis are present
- intent/proposal context is linkable
- effect classification is explicit (`internal` vs `external`)
- outcome and reason code are stable
- evidence links remain integrity-verifiable

## Ontology alignment

Traceability is plane-aware:
- `core`: authority and lifecycle decision traces
- `exec`: execution/effect traces
- `brain`: cognitive proposal and reconfiguration traces

Historical package labels are not required to reconstruct truth.
