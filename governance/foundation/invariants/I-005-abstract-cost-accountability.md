# I-005 — Abstract Cost Accountability

Cost accountability is required for governed transitions.

## Invariant

Each valid transition MUST be attributable to declared cost dimensions in a versioned metric space.

## Required properties

- metric space identifier/version is declared
- transition-level linkage to cost evidence exists
- external effects include risk dimension
- cost evidence is verifiable offline

## Ontology alignment

- `core` governs admissibility and policy relevance of cost
- `exec` emits execution/effect cost evidence
- `brain` emits cognitive-cost evidence where it affects governed transitions
